What Do We Offer?

Our Club Helps Students Take Professional Photos About Their University Life

Venue: ATB Cellar (#1 Room, Right Side)

Our Motives

We are a group of professional photographers who help and teach university students capturing the best moments of their personal and academic life in a professional level. Our lessons are mainly practical, and theoretical part gives tips of photographing. We conduct our classes in a convenient day and time, and during classes students practice and learn how to shoot photos even with their mobile phones. Spending free time after a long and stressful day capturing exciting photos give students enthusiasm and joy. Photo Club makes the social life of WIUT students delightful and interactive.
